WanderWallet

Social and cheap travelling

Ever had the opportunity to travel abroad, going from one city to another like all the adventurous backpacker inside us? Then you must have experienced, what a drag it can be, when you don't know anyone that can help you, or don't know how to go from one city to another in a fast but cheap way. Although all these things might be the salt and pepper to your adventure, we bet you will have more fun if you know your way around a city like a local do.

So we welcome you to WanderWallet, the tool you plan your trip with, travel with and socialize with.

Our objective as the WanderWallet team, is to make sure you don't end up wasting time to plan your trip when you could be seeing all the beautiful stuff you are supposed to whilst travelling. And make sure you experience a city like a local, seeing places hidden to the ordinary tourists' eyes, tasting authentic food and getting the most out of your precious trip abroad.

Feed

Choose from tours curated for you

Holiday season is coming, but you have no time to plan ahead for your awesome trip? Well, you can start checking out the tours that are curated for you based on your taste. These are basically tours created depending on your interests, previous trips and ratings on other tours.

Or do you have a specific kind of vacation in mind? A trip to the Basque country perhaps, travelling city by city experience every inch of the Basque cuisine? Then you might also take a look at some of the tours designed by our talented team or members of the WanderWallet community with more specific themes in mind such as food, museums, beaches and a lot more.

Just go to the feed tab, choose from the presented tours and decide if it fits your needs.

Discover New Places

Create custom tours using the map

Well you might want to be in total control of your tour too. Then just go to the discover tab and see all the locations that you can visit using the WanderWallet App. Start adding them with just one click and see where your trip takes you.

You can also help the WanderWallet community by designing your own tours, or taking an active role in rating tours created by other people. Tours with greater ratings will help the community increase the satisfaction one gets out of their vacation.

Calendar

We will schedule your trip

If you are done choosing the places you want to go, its time to plan what to do in these locations. But don't worry, we also got that for you.

For every city you choose on the map, the app will generate a schedule customized for you. This schedule will include museum trips, events that you might be interested in, restaurants or bars you might want to go to.

Also a selection of cheap transportation including railway, bus, plane (and other ways of possible commute) between selected cities will be available for you choose. This selection of transportation will dynamically change if you choose to not go to one of the selected cities.

However, if you don't like what we have to offer, you can always change and adjust this schedule whenever and wherever you want, making the schedule felixable and adjustable to any kind of situation.

Local Guides

Consult local guides or become one

It's always better to know someone who is from the place you are travelling to. They know how to get around. They know the cheap and also the hip places to be. They know what to eat and where to eat. Basically they have all the information you need to have a fun vacation without all those boring touristy activities.

So as the WanderWallet team, we aim to bring the local and the tourists together to make sure everyone has a better time.

As a user of the app, you can become a local guide just by applying and verifying where you live. This will let you socialize and help people visiting your hometown. All you have to do is to wait for some foreigners to contact you.

Chat

Socialize with everyone using the app

The main tool to socialize with the WanderWallet community is the inbox where you can send messages to local guides or other users and receive instant messages.

Using this feature you will be able to contact local guides of the places you are planning to visit and ask them questions to get the vital information you need for the time before you travel and while you are travelling.

You will also be able contact people visiting the same locations as you are, provided they confirm your request to communicate. Thus making it easier to find company to experience the trip with.
